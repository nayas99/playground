About Us

Anandam is a Kolkata-based NGO run by a group of passionate individuals. We are committed to supporting underprivileged communities through clothing drives, flood relief efforts, and more.

Since 2017, we have served over 5000 children and 1000 families with your love and support.

What We Do

Clothing for Puja

Every Durga Puja, we distribute new clothes to poor children, helping them celebrate with joy.

Flood Relief

In times of disaster, we provide food, clothes, and essentials to affected families in rural areas.
